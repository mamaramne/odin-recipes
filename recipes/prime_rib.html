Prime Ribs Recipe

If you've ever carved into a prime rib at a big holiday meal expecting a juicy,

perfectly pink interior only to discover a dry, overcooked roast, this recipe is for you.

Here are some tips from Chef Tin to get you started:

  • 1 prime rib
  • 1/4 unsalted butter
  • 1 freshly grounded black pepper
  • 1 herb
  • kosher salt
  1. gather ingredients
  2. combine ingredients
  3. roast prime rib
  4. turn off oven
  5. remove prime rib from oven
  6. enjoy
